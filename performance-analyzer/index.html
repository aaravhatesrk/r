Every rep, every split, one place.

Athlyze is a standalone performance log for any sport — running, swimming, cycling, lifting, basketball, football, tennis or anything else you train for. Log a metric, and Athlyze tracks your personal bests and charts your trend automatically. Everything is stored locally in this browser — no account, no server, nothing leaves your device.

Log a performance entry

Pick a sport and a metric — presets are pre-filled with sensible units, or choose "Custom metric" for anything not listed.

Personal bests

One card per sport + metric you've logged, with the best value on record and how many times you've logged it.

Progress over time

Pick a sport + metric you've logged more than once to see the trend line and the computed improvement since your first entry.

Log at least two entries for the same sport and metric to see a trend.

History

Athlyze — a standalone sports-performance analyzer built alongside the Rooted business plan prototype. All data is stored in this browser's localStorage only; nothing is sent to a server. Sample rows are pre-loaded on first visit and can be removed from History.
