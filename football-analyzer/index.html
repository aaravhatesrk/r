Review the tape. Understand the mistake. Fix it this week.

PitchIQ is a standalone coaching-critique tool for football (soccer) players and coaches: upload your own match or training clip, or just describe a passage of play in your own words — from your own game, a teammate's, or one you watched at any level up to the professional/international game. PitchIQ matches what you describe against a coached knowledge base of common technical, tactical and decision-making mistakes, and gives you back why it happens, how to fix it, and a drill to practice.

Honest about what this is. PitchIQ does not use computer vision to "watch" your video for you — that needs real video-analysis infrastructure this dependency-free, static tool doesn't have. Instead, it gives you a structured framework: play the clip yourself, tag what you see against a published mistake library, and get instant, rule-based coaching feedback for each tag — same transparent approach as Rooted's Wellness Advisor, not AI or a professional scouting service. Your video file stays entirely on your device; it is never uploaded anywhere, and it isn't saved between visits — only the notes and timestamps you tag are.
